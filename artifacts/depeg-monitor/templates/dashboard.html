About

StablecoinWatch monitors major stablecoins for depeg events every 5 minutes using CoinGecko's public API.

Monitored: USDT, USDC, DAI, FRAX, TUSD, PYUSD, cNGN, bNGN

A depeg is triggered when price deviates ≥1% from the target peg.

African stablecoins (cNGN, bNGN) are tracked against the implied USD/NGN rate.

Telegram Bot: Get real-time alerts directly to your phone when a depeg is detected. Use /subscribe in the bot.
